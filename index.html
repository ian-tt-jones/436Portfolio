Introduction

This semester has introduced me to the depth and complexity that exists in data visualization. Prior to this semester I had no idea the amount of ways there were to visualize data. I did not know many forms of visualizations there were besides the trivial types we all grew up learning (bar graph, pie chart, line chart), but after having experienced this class, working with my peers, and seeing how far the mind can go to come up with ways to portray data I feel like a much more creative person. To sum up my experience in this class I wanted to create a personal site for my portfolio to demonstrate my learning this semester. You can use the navbar at the top of the page to see the topics from this semester that I chose to reflect on and refine.
